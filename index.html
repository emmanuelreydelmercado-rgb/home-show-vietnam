Our Event

The annual International Houseware and Gift Fair Vietnam - Home Show Vietnam, was established in 2023 with great success, it is a great opportunity for you to negotiate business with high-quality houseware & gift manufacturers, exporters, importers, distributors, and buyers from different regions around the world.

Home Show Vietnam 2026 will adopt an innovative offline + online exhibition model, allowing buyers to select more products and communicate with exhibitors with one click without being limited by time and distance, truly experiencing the diversity of exhibitions, and helping to achieve cooperation in multi-dimensions.
